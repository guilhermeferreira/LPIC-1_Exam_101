Video: Install and remove packages with YUM

---------------------------------------

RPM manages packages. rpm is the Package Management tool. Equivalent to dpkg
in Debian/Ubuntu.
  rpm (Red Hat) = dpkg (Debian)

YUM manages package's repositories. yum is the Repository Management tool.
Equivalent to apt in Debian/Ubuntu.
  yum/dnf (Red Hat) = apt (Debian)
